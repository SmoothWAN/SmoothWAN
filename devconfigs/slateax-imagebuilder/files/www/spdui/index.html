Quick Setup:

1. Plug at least one WAN with internet access into LAN2 or WAN (labels on the back of the router).
2. You can also connect to two Wi-Fi stations in "Simple Wi-Fi Setup" in the menu bar above.
3. This device is capable of using up-to 2 Wi-Fi stations while simultaneously broadcasting 6 APs.
4. Navigate the menu bar above to VPN->Speedify to install Speedify and configure.
5. Speedify UI ports label: LAN2: eth1, WAN: eth0.
6. Change administration password at System -> Administration.
